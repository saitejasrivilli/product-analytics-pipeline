Key Insights → Product Decisions

📈 Monday peaks at 27K users (18% above weekly avg, 61% reorder rate). In a Meta ads context: FMCG advertisers should weight Monday budgets higher — maps directly to automated campaign pacing decisions.
⭐ Banana: 88% reorder rate across 18.7K orders signals habitual behavior — the data analog of DAU/MAU in social products. Auto-add or subscription feature for top-10 sticky products reduces friction across 60K+ weekly reorders.
🔄 59.86% overall reorder rate = 6 in 10 purchases are repeat behavior. Cold-start problem affects only 40% of catalog — personalization infrastructure ROI is high. Mirrors feature retention analysis for Facebook/Instagram product decisions.
📦 10.6 avg items per order maps to session depth in feed products. Users above this threshold show improved retention curves — same principle applies to optimizing feed session length targets in Reels or Stories.
🎯 Wednesday 41% below Monday in demand. Inventory surplus opportunity — targeted Wednesday promotions flatten demand curve. In Meta ads: low-demand periods = underpriced ad inventory, signal for reach campaign optimization.
